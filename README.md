## Tomato Rice
Ingredients

. 1 Cup Rice
. 2 Tomatoes
. 1 Onion
. Salt
. Oil
. Green Chilli

## Steps

1. Wash and cook the rice.
2. Chop tomatoes and onions.
3. Heat oil in a pan.
4. Add onions and fry until golden.
5. Add tomatoes,chilli and salt.
6. Cook until soft.
7. Mix with cooked rice.
8. Serve Hot.
